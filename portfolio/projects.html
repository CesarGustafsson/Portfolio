ABOUT PROJECTS

POSTER

This was a school project created for the UN Day, where we designed posters to promote the seminar. I chose to work in the Bauhaus style to convey a sense of seriousness and innovation, qualities associated with the UN. To capture attention, I incorporated bold colors and geometric shapes while maintaining a consistent structure.

I aimed for a dynamic composition by carefully placing text and elements in a crossing layout, enhancing the visual movement and reinforcing the Bauhaus aesthetic.

CPR COUNCIL

This project focused on designing a prototype for Hjärtstartarregistret, a government service that helps users locate nearby defibrillators. Since our program covers a broad range of topics, I’m not yet a Figma expert, but I see room for growth and have developed a strong interest in the tool, as I enjoy design.

I aimed for consistency by maintaining uniform border radius, stroke, colors, and layout wherever possible, making the interface more predictable and user-friendly.

You can check out the official service here: hjartstartarregistret.se to compare with my approach.

YAPPY BIRD

In this pair project, we developed a voice-controlled Flappy Bird clone using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Instead of tapping, players produce sounds, like claps or whistles, to make the bird flap, creating a unique and engaging interaction.

We designed pixelated visuals similar to the original game to leverage affordances and recognition, making it instantly familiar. To enhance discoverability, we ensured real-time feedback through visual and audio cues, reinforcing the connection between input and action.
